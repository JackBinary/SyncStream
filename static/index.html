Privacy & Terms

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. Here's what you need to know:

  • No persistent data storage - Everything happens in memory only
  • IP addresses are temporarily used for rate limiting to prevent abuse
  • Nicknames, chat messages, and video URLs are never saved to disk
  • Room data is deleted automatically when the last person leaves
  • No cookies, no tracking, no analytics

In short: We don't keep any information about you other than what's needed to make the site work while you're using it.
